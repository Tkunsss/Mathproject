Math project

This is a simple math project where you can drag and drop boxes to the correct columns. The boxes are randomly generated and can be dragged and dropped anywhere on the screen. When you're done, click the "Start Animation" button to see the boxes move to their correct columns. Click the "Reset" button to reset all the boxes and columns.
